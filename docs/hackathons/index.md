## Monthly Schedule

We host hackathons monthly, alternating between different AI/ML challenges. Each event runs Friday evening through Sunday afternoon (approximately 48 hours).

**Typical Format:**
- **Friday:** 5 PM - 10 PM (Kickoff, team formation, planning)
- **Saturday:** 9 AM - 8 PM (Full day building with mentor support)
- **Sunday:** 9 AM - 4 PM (Final development, presentations, awards)
